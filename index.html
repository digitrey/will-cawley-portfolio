Bio

Hi there! Will here. Thanks for visiting my website!

I work with software to figure out where it breaks the experience for a customer. Finding those gaps in understanding — where the user wants to assume the fetal position and cry — is what I live for.

I hold a Master of Arts in technical communication with a specialty in usability, user experience (UX), and human-centered design. In addition, I have earned two additional degrees in applied sociology (Criminology).

My work experience includes documenting emerging technologies for the public and private sector, such as 3D printers, RFID machines, and graphical (G) programming languages. It also includes lots of application programming interfaces (API). This work has led me to collaborate with diverse teams from around the world, including India, China, and Romania.

Through my work, I also became experienced with Agile development methods. I eventually became a certified Scrum Master. With this skill set, I coached writing and development teams to success. This included removing impediments that stood between the teams and their goals.
